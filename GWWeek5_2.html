A programmer, developer, dev, coder, or software engineer is a person who creates computer software.

The term computer programmer can refer to a specialist in one area of computers or to a generalist who writes code for many kinds of software.

A range of occupations, including: software developer, web developer, mobile applications developer, embedded firmware developer, software engineer, computer scientist, or software analyst, while they do involve programming, also require a range of other skills.

The use of the simple term programmer for these positions is sometimes considered an insulting or derogatory simplification
